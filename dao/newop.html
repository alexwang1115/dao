Rose Box Start-up New Opportunities

New Customer Segment and Collaboration , April 7, 2021

We found that the China international student is not sensitive to gift price. We collaborate with another flower studio to launch a floral workshop. We corporate with another complementary store to improve the sale.

New Customer Segment

We introduce the their product to a new customer segment. We found that the China international student is not sensitive for gift price so we help the start up get into this market The purpose we change the color of the background, and the styles is pursuit the younger consumer. Because, above 45% of the exist customer are older than 35 years old. We believe the younger segment is more profitable and increase sales. We mainly introduce their product to Chinese international student segment by launching a Wechat account. Because, im knowledgable about how to operate Wechat store, and I have a lot of potential leads in wechat. During 4month, 6000+CAD sales issued in Wechat.

Collaboration

We collaborate with another flower studio to launch a floral workshop, which teach student to make floral. They don’t have their official website, they are a studio and operating in WeChat and Chinese community, called YoYo flower studio.

We corporate with other complementary store to improve the sale. For example We put our product in cake store. They can sell us product and earn commission, because we believe that if the customer want to buy a cake for special days, that will have 80%possibility to buy a flower

We also did the flyers promotion (Distributed in front of shopping mall)
